# Custom Spacer - Better Adhesion

**Type**: 3D Printable accessory
**Purpose**: Provides larger adhesive surface for Nuki mounting
**Nuki Model**: All models

---

## The Problem

Sometimes Nuki's adhesive mounting doesn't hold well due to:
- Small contact area
- Uneven door surface
- Door rosette shape

---

## The Solution

A 3D printed **spacer plate** that provides:
- Larger adhesive surface
- Level mounting surface
- Better weight distribution

## Print Settings

| Setting | Value |
|---------|-------|
| **Material** | PLA or PETG |
| **Layer height** | 0.2mm |
| **Infill** | 40% |
| **Walls** | 3 |

## When to Use

- Door has small rosette
- Adhesive keeps failing
- Uneven door surface
- Want extra security on mounting
