## Summary

Sweden uses **Scandinavian oval cylinders**, which are different from European euro cylinders. Nuki made a specific **Smart Lock Ultra Nordics** model for this market.

---

## Lock Types in Sweden

### Standard: Scandinavian Oval Cylinder

Swedish locks use **oval-shaped cylinders** (SS standard), not the euro profile used in most of Europe.


## Installation Considerations

### Bostadsrätt (Condo/Apartment)

- **Bostadsrättsförening rules**: Check with your BRF
- Main doors in apartment buildings may have restrictions
- Your own apartment door usually OK

### Villa (House)

- Full freedom to install
- Consider if door has ASSA or Abloy cylinder
- Measure cylinder length before ordering

### Hyresrätt (Rental)

- **Get landlord permission**
- Keep original cylinder
- Reinstall when moving out

---

## Security Standards

Sweden has high security standards for locks. The Universal Cylinder meets:
- SS 3522 (Swedish standard)
- EN 1303 (European standard)

---

## Climate Considerations

- **Cold weather**: Nuki rated for indoor use, protected from direct cold
- **Battery life**: May reduce in very cold conditions
- Keep spare batteries during winter
