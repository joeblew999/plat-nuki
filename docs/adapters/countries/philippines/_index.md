# Philippines - Nuki Compatibility Guide

**Status**: ⚠️ UNOFFICIAL (Mixed market)
**Nuki Model**: Smart Lock (US) for deadbolts, Smart Lock Pro for euro cylinders
**Confidence**: Medium

---

## Summary

The Philippines has **both US and European lock types** due to historical US influence. Deadbolts are common in houses, euro cylinders in condos. Check your lock type first.

---

## Installation Considerations

### Condos

- **DMCI, Ayala, etc.**: Often euro cylinders
- **Building rules**: Check with admin
- **Fire doors**: Don't modify

### Houses

- **Subdivisions**: Often US-style deadbolts
- **Gate locks**: Nuki doesn't fit gates
- **Grilles**: Common - Nuki goes on main door
