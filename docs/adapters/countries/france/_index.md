## Summary

France uses **euro profile cylinders** (NF standard). Official Nuki market with French-language support.

## Insurance (Assurance)

French insurers may have lock requirements. Check:
- A2P certification on your existing cylinder
- Nuki doesn't replace the cylinder (Pro model)
- Inform your assureur
