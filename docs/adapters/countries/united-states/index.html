Summary

Nuki entered the US market in July 2025. The Smart Lock (US) is designed specifically for American deadbolt locks and includes 3 adapters in the box.

Installation

Requirements

  • Door type: Must have separate deadbolt (not knob-in-lock)
  • Deadbolt center to frame: > 1.38" (35mm) for inward doors
  • Deadbolt center to handle: > 1.38" (35mm)

Steps

  1. Remove existing thumbturn interior escutcheon
  2. Identify your tailpiece shape (square, D, or slotted)
  3. Select matching adapter
  4. Mount Nuki over existing deadbolt
  5. Pair with app

Compatibility Notes

Works With

  • Standard single-cylinder deadbolts
  • Most multi-point locks with deadbolt section
  • ANSI/BHMA grade 1, 2, or 3 locks

Does NOT Work With

  • Knob-only locks (no separate deadbolt)
  • Mortise locks (European style)
  • Smart locks with built-in electronics
  • Rim locks
